Distance-2 Coloring Game

Learn the game by playing it.

Pick a preset graph, choose whether you want to act as Alice or Bob, and play against the solver. A color is blocked if the same color already appears on the selected vertex, a neighbor, or a neighbor-of-a-neighbor.

Your role

How To Read The Game

Alice wants every vertex to be colored legally. Bob wins as soon as even one uncolored vertex has no color left.

Glossary

  • Vertex: one point in the graph.
  • Distance 2: up to two edges away.
  • Dead vertex: an uncolored vertex with no allowed color left.
